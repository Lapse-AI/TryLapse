## What works well (show partners this)

1. **Command center uses real data** — 2m 08s time-to-scorecard, 14% flake, 18-run trend; no Acme mock runs in list.
2. **Run detail is the hero** — 3×5 matrix with pass/partial; 9 marketing-ready delights; 10-agent trace with crawl → workflow → journeys.
3. **Compare tells a release story** — `Red → Green`, pages 0→16, resolved vs new issues (great for “what changed since Tuesday?”).
4. **Human loop** — Agree / Disagree / False positive on findings; one issue already shows `agreed` chip.
5. **Self-serve path** — Init wizard writes YAML to `artifacts/configs/`; recommendations export for Linear handoff.
