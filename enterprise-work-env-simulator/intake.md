# Intake: Synthetic Company Product Rehearsal

*Updated 2026-05-28 — primary ICP flipped to pre-customer builders*

## Product (one sentence)

Synthetic company + persona agents exercise **your product** end-to-end (UX, journeys, functionality, integration and compliance signals) and return a readiness scorecard — **before** real customers or POC.

## Primary ICP

- Founder / PM / builder shipping B2B SaaS or tools with **few or no users yet**
- Pain: “How do I know it works or what’s broken without beta users?”

## Secondary ICP

- B2B SaaS Solutions Engineering — rehearse prospect POC (same engine, later)

## The tool being evaluated

**Primary:** The **builder’s own product** (website/app).  
**Secondary:** Vendor product inside prospect stack (SE motion).

## Problem

Shipping blind — manual dogfooding doesn’t scale; human user tests are slow; scripted E2E misses persona judgment and org context.

## Known adjacent competitors

Veris, VEI (agent/twin gyms) · UserTesting, Maze (human) · Demostack (vendor demos) · Playwright + CI (scripted only)

## MVP test (week 1)

One URL · 3 personas · 5 journeys · automated scorecard — validate with founders, not SE procurement.
