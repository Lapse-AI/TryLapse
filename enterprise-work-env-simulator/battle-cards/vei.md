# Battle Card: VEI (Virtual Enterprise Interface)

*Skill: startup-competitors | Generated: 2026-05-28*

## Who they are

OSS **programmable enterprise digital twin**: Slack, Jira, Graph, Salesforce, missions, contracts, benchmarks, what-if branches ([strangeloopcanon/vei](https://github.com/strangeloopcanon/vei)).

## Strengths

- **Deterministic** core — reproducible evals (same seed = same world)
- Provider-shaped APIs (Twin Gateway)
- Crisis missions + contract scorecards
- Historical what-if from real bundles (Enron example)

## Weaknesses

- Early OSS — small community vs MiroFish
- Not packaged as **commercial GTM** for SE teams
- LLM NPC mode optional — less “human chaos” out of box

## How to win

- **Don’t rebuild** — extend/fork with **GraphRAG ingest** + **SE workflow UI**
- Sell **vertical packs + calibration studies** OSS can’t monetize alone
- Add **parallel persona swarms** where VEI is template-deterministic

## When they win

- Buyer wants **free**, self-hosted, developer-led
- Needs **provable replay** for agent benchmarks
- Security forbids third-party SaaS

## Vulnerability

Productization gap — no Demostack-grade **sales UI** or **prospect-specific spin-up in 2 clicks**.

## Churn signals

Teams that need **managed compliance** may outgrow DIY OSS.
